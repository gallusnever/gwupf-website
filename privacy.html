Privacy

Last updated: January 2025

The Short Version

No cookies. No tracking pixels. No personal data collection unless you use a tool that requires it (like the ASC 842 calculator's terms acceptance). Even then, we keep it minimal.

What We Collect

  • Most tools: Nothing. They run in your browser.
  • ASC 842 calculator: When you accept terms, we log a hash (not your name), timestamp, and calculation inputs for performance monitoring.
  • Server logs: Standard web server logs (IP, timestamp, page requested) auto-deleted after 30 days.

What We Don't Do

  • No cookies or tracking
  • No selling or sharing data
  • No marketing emails
  • No third-party analytics

Why We Log Anything

For tools requiring terms acceptance: proof of acceptance and basic usage monitoring to improve the tools.

Data Retention

  • Server logs: 30 days
  • Terms acceptance logs: 1 year
  • Calculation logs: 90 days

Your Rights

Want your data deleted? Email delete@getwhatupayfor.tools with your approximate usage date/time. We'll remove what we can find.

Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update the date at the top. No sneaky stuff.

Questions?

Intentionally no contact info. These are personal tools shared publicly, not a business.
